WHO ARE WE

"Don't Just Dreck It!' is mainly a waste management project with an aim to implement the 3R principle Recycle,Reuse,Reduce Trash!

We are a team of motivated computer science students who aim to bring a change in the environment for the better tomorrow.

Our Project Will try to increase the dependency on Recycle & Reuse of the products Rather than just Trashing them away.
