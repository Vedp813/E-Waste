E-WASTE

In India, the quantity of “e-waste” or electronic waste has now become a major problem. Disposal of e-waste is an emerging global environmental and public health issue, as this waste has become the most rapidly growing segment of the formal municipal waste stream in the world.

DONATE

You may not have use for the electronic device anymore, but that does not mean it is of no use to others! .If you feel like you’ve had the item for some time and would like a change, why not pass the old one to someone who needs it? Donating these items for reuse prolongs their lives and thus conserves our natural resources.

INCENTIVE

So the quick changing and advancement in electronics has now lead to the problem of the management of the waste as people don't know the value of their used products so we are trying to give them a good incentive for the reusable electronics.

WHO ARE WE

"Don't Just Dreck It!' is mainly a waste management project with an aim to implement the 3R principle Recycle,Reuse,Reduce Trash!

We are a team of motivated computer science students who aim to bring a change in the environment for the better tomorrow.

Our Project Will try to increase the dependency on Recycle & Reuse of the products Rather than just Trashing them away.
